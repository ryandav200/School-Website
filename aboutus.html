ABOUT US

Shachah International Christian School is a Christian founded school teaching the Accelerated Christian Education curriculum, west of Kampala located in Busega Kibumbiro. Founded in 2016, starting out as a home school which later grew into a formally registered ACE school.

Shachah is committed to providing relevant Christian based learning opportunities.

Under the Accelerated Christian Education curriculum We offer classes from Pre-School to Grade 12. We also equip our learners with Financial literacy where We teach children to do business through our financial literacy model.

We have daily Devotions, Ministry and encourage our learners to engage in concern for community.

Our learners also have classes in French, Art and designs, Reading, Home science, Urban Farming, Swimming, Computer, Website designs and Music which includes Singing, dancing, instruments, speech, Acting.

Mission

To support and mentor Shachah learners to discover and live their God given purpose.

Vision

A Shachah learner attaining their God given potential.
